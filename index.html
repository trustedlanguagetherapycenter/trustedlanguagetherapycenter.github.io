Special Education

Fine Motor Skills

Include reaching, grasping and manipulating objects with your hands. Its also involve vision, specifically often referred to hand-eye coordination.

Gross Motor Skills

Involve movements of the large muscles of the arms, legs, and torso. People who struggle with gross motor skills have trouble doing running and jumping.
