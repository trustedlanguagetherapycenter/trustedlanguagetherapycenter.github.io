About Dr. Chandni Jain

A RCI Certified speech and behavior therapist with 10 years of experience typically has a wealth of expertise in addressing a wide range of communication, cognitive, and behavioral challenges in clients of various age groups.

Skills and Expertise

  • Enhancing language development in children and adults with developmental delays or neurological conditions.
  • Implementing augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) methods for non-verbal clients.
  • Developing and implementing behavior intervention plans (BIPs) for clients with autism spectrum disorder (ASD), ADHD, and other behavioral challenges.
  • Applying evidence-based practices such as Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) to encourage positive behaviors and reduce problematic ones.

Experience Highlights

  • Successfully treated clients with a variety of needs, including developmental delays, learning disabilities, and social communication challenges.
  • Conducted individualized assessments and developed tailored therapy plans.
  • Collaborated with multidisciplinary teams, including educators, psychologists, and occupational therapists.
  • Provided training and workshops for parents, caregivers, and educators.
